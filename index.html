Research Interests

My main research interests lie between differential geometry and mathematical physics. My work so far has been especially focused on Poisson geometry and related structures, such as Lie algebroids, Lie groupoids and higher analogues. In particular, in my thesis I use simplicial methods for the study of Lie n-groupoids, higher vector bundles over these and shifted symplectic structures.
